Our Vision/Mision

The consecrated family was birthed through burden from the Lord. "For,behold,the darkness shall cover the earth,and gross darkness the people:but the Lord shall arise upon thee,and his glory shall be seen upon thee.And the gentiles shall come to thy light,and kings to the brightness of thy rising". The Lord made us realise that for this to come to fulfillment there is a need for men who will delibrately consecrate themselves to Him compleletely. And as we trust the to Lord respond to His Heartbeat,He gave us a word of assurance as a vision to run with.

"...for the earth shall be full of the Knowledge of the Lord, as the water cover the sea,Isaiah11:9b." As we pursue this cause,we are more than sure that the knowledge of the Lord will cover the earth as water cover the sea.Because the Lord will not do less than His word...
